Meet all the Ewamon

In these slideshow are all the Ewamon. We have a total of 13 Ewamon and all of them have a different typing. The Ewamon also have different attacks that are based on their typing. Players can see that because of the names of the moves and the color that shows because every typing has a color that goes with them. When signing up you will pick a starter and get some money. After that go to the shop and buy Ewamon to collect them all. If you want to collect them all you to win allot of battles to earn money.

Introduction to the battle system

Invite your friends and challenge them to a battle with your team of Ewamon. You can choose which ever Ewamon you want as your active Ewamon to battle with. You also have the options to heal for one turn during a battle. If it is not possible to play anymore or you see no hope in the game you can always forfeit. When forfeiting the opponent will earn a small amount of money. Winning a battle will earn you some money. Keep a winning streak and aim for the top of the Ewamon ranking list. Doing so will show everyone who the real Ewamon master is.
